How AI Understands Context & Meaning

What is Attention Mechanism?

The Attention Mechanism is the secret sauce that allows Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT to understand the context of words in a sentence.

Think of it as the AI's ability to "pay attention" to relevant words when trying to understand what you mean. Just like how humans naturally focus on important parts of a sentence, LLMs use attention to figure out which words matter most.

Without attention, AI would treat every word in isolation, completely missing the nuances of language!

How Attention Scores Are Calculated

The attention scores are calculated using the Dot Product of word vectors. When two words are related, their vectors point in similar directions, resulting in a HIGH dot product. Unrelated words have vectors pointing in different directions, giving a LOW dot product.
